## Posting Schedule Recommendations

### Optimal Posting Times

**Facebook:**
- Weekdays: 12-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- Weekends: 12-1 PM

**Instagram:**
- Weekdays: 11 AM-1 PM, 7-9 PM
- Weekends: 10 AM-2 PM

**Twitter/X:**
- Weekdays: 9 AM, 12 PM, 5 PM
- Post 3-5 times per day

**LinkedIn:**
- Tuesday-Thursday: 7-8 AM, 12 PM, 5-6 PM
- Avoid weekends

### Posting Frequency

**New Property:**
- Post on all platforms within 24 hours
- Repost weekly until under contract

**Price Reduction:**
- Immediate post on all platforms
- Highlight the price drop

**Under Contract:**
- Update status on all platforms
- Thank followers for engagement

**Sold:**
- Celebration post
- Highlight success story
- Encourage others to reach out

## Content Customization Tips

### When to Customize

1. **Special Features**: Property has unique features worth highlighting
2. **Price Reduction**: Emphasize the savings
3. **Open House**: Add date and time
4. **Urgent Sale**: Highlight deadline or special circumstances
5. **Multiple Properties**: Batch similar properties together

### What to Keep Consistent

1. **Contact Information**: Always 910.363.6147
2. **Website**: Always USAHUDhomes.com
3. **Company Name**: Lightkeeper Realty
4. **Experience**: 25 years as Registered HUD Buyer's Agency
5. **Owner-Occupant Incentives**: Core benefits

### What to Customize

1. **Opening Hook**: Make it attention-grabbing
2. **Property Highlights**: Unique features
3. **Call to Action**: Urgency or special offer
4. **Hashtags**: Location-specific tags
5. **Tone**: Match platform audience

## Image Guidelines

### Image Requirements

**Facebook:**
- Minimum: 1200 x 630 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Format: JPG or PNG

**Instagram:**
- Square: 1080 x 1080 pixels (recommended)
- Portrait: 1080 x 1350 pixels
- Landscape: 1080 x 566 pixels
- Format: JPG or PNG

**Twitter/X:**
- Minimum: 1200 x 675 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 16:9
- Format: JPG or PNG

**LinkedIn:**
- Minimum: 1200 x 627 pixels
- Aspect ratio: 1.91:1
- Format: JPG or PNG

### Image Best Practices

1. **Quality**: Use high-resolution images
2. **Lighting**: Bright, well-lit photos
3. **Angle**: Show property's best features
4. **Staging**: Clean, decluttered spaces
5. **Multiple Shots**: Exterior and interior
6. **Branding**: Consider watermark with logo

## Engagement Tips

### Responding to Comments

**Positive Comments:**
- Thank them for interest
- Offer to provide more information
- Invite them to schedule showing

**Questions:**
- Respond within 24 hours
- Provide accurate information
- Offer to discuss via phone

**Negative Comments:**
- Stay professional
- Address concerns politely
- Take conversation private if needed

### Building Engagement

1. **Ask Questions**: "What features are you looking for?"
2. **Share Success Stories**: "Another happy homeowner!"
3. **Educational Content**: "HUD Home Buying Tips"
4. **Behind the Scenes**: "Property walkthrough video"
5. **Client Testimonials**: Share positive reviews

## Tracking Performance

### Metrics to Monitor

**Facebook:**
- Reach and impressions
- Engagement (likes, comments, shares)
- Click-through rate to property page

**Instagram:**
- Likes and comments
- Saves and shares
- Profile visits
- Link clicks (in bio)

**Twitter/X:**
- Impressions
- Engagement rate
- Link clicks
- Retweets

**LinkedIn:**
- Views
- Engagement
- Click-through rate
- Follower growth

### Success Indicators

1. **High Engagement**: Comments and shares
2. **Website Traffic**: Clicks to property page
3. **Lead Generation**: Inquiries via phone/form
4. **Follower Growth**: Increasing audience
5. **Property Interest**: Multiple inquiries

## Common Mistakes to Avoid

1. **Inconsistent Posting**: Post regularly
2. **Poor Image Quality**: Use high-res photos
3. **Missing Contact Info**: Always include phone
4. **Ignoring Comments**: Respond promptly
5. **Wrong Timing**: Post during peak hours
6. **Too Much Text**: Keep it concise
7. **No Call to Action**: Tell them what to do
8. **Forgetting Hashtags**: Use relevant tags
9. **Not Tracking**: Monitor performance
10. **Outdated Info**: Keep status current
